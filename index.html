About Us

We're a small travel company based in Shimla, running tours in Himachal since 2020. Started by locals who know these mountains well.

Our tours focus on Spiti Valley, Manali, and surrounding areas. We work with experienced drivers and arrange stays in reliable hotels and guesthouses. Groups are usually small, between 6-12 people.

We prefer working with local homestays and family-run places when possible. Most of our bookings come from referrals.
