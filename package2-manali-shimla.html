Arrival at Phillaur to Manali - Overnight Journey

Arrival at Manali - Solang Valley || Rohtang Pass || Sissu Village || Atal Tunnel

The day is scheduled for enjoying the sights and experiences at Solang Valley and Rohtang Pass. Around 14 km from the Manali city, Beas Kund and Solang Village, lies the Solang Valley, renowned for its scenic beauty and known for hosting finest adventure activities like Skiing, Trekking, Paragliding, Skating, Zorbing, Parachuting. Then you will explore the Rohtang Pass, the highest mountain pass. Enjoy the bustling tourist activity, taste the local renditions, enjoy the cool breeze and more. Visit Sissu village, Koksar Village and Atal Tunnel. Dinner and stay overnight at the hotel.

Manali Transfer to Shimla via Kullu

Today after breakfast, check out from the hotel, as it's time to say goodbye to Manali. Start your journey towards Shimla. Shimla is almost 250 km away from Manali and it will take 5 to 6 hours. On the way you will visit places like Sundernagar Lake, Pandoh Dam, Aut Tunnel and Kullu Valley. On reaching Kullu, visit Mata Vaishno Devi Temple. Continue your journey towards Shimla. As you reach, check in directly at your hotel, take a welcome tea/coffee. Settle into the room. Enjoy your delicious dinner and comfort stay at the hotel.

Shimla Sightseeing and Half Day Tour to Kufri

Morning get fresh up and then have breakfast with the view of mountains. After having your breakfast gear up for the day sightseeing, as you are going to explore Kufri - a perfect day out. Located 16 km from Shimla, Kufri is bestowed with slopes, which makes it a hub for adventure lovers. A great place to unwind, Kufri offers panoramic view of the Himalayas. This tiny tinsel town features various places of interest like Himalayan National Park, Chini Bungalow, Kufri Fun World, etc. The highest peak in Kufri - Mahasu Peak is yet another hotspot for tourists. You can do activities like Yak Riding, Pony Ride. Afternoon get transfer to Shimla, visit Mall Road, The Ridge, Jakhoo Temple, Christ Church. Enjoy evening at Mall Road, have shopping and dinner. Overnight stay at Hotel.

Shimla to Delhi

After the breakfast at the hotel check out from the hotel and start your drive to Delhi. Delhi is around 370 km from Shimla and will take 7 to 8 hours journey. As you reach check-in to your hotel, dinner and overnight stay at hotel.

Delhi Local Sightseeing

After the breakfast at the hotel check out from the hotel and visit Delhi. You will be leaving for Delhi Sightseeing. Go for shopping, take some casual strolls, and relish some local street foods. After that start your journey towards Delhi Railway Station to start your journey way back to home.
